## 👋 About Me

Full-stack engineer building production systems end to end — from React/Next.js frontends down to the APIs, queues, and databases behind them. I move comfortably between **Ruby on Rails**, **Node**, **Django**, and **Go**, and pick microservices or a monolith based on what the problem actually needs, not what's trendy.

- 🔭 &nbsp; Building backend systems & APIs across **Ruby on Rails**, **Node**, **Django**, **MERN**, and **Go**.
- 🧩 &nbsp; Comfortable designing both **microservices** (Kafka, gRPC) and **monolith** architectures.
- 📦 &nbsp; Recent work: blogging platforms with subscriptions, real-time notification services (Redis Streams), commute/earnings calculators, and file-upload pipelines (S3).
- ☁️ &nbsp; Deploying & scaling on **AWS**.
- 🌱 &nbsp; Currently exploring distributed systems, caching strategies, and event-driven architectures.
- ⚡ &nbsp; I ship fast and I like my systems (and my READMEs) clean.

## ⚙️ How I Build

- 🧱 &nbsp; **End to end.** Frontend, API, data layer, and infra — I own the whole slice, not just one tier.
- 🧪 &nbsp; **Pragmatic architecture.** Microservices when the boundaries earn their cost, a monolith when they don't.
- 🚀 &nbsp; **Production-minded.** Caching, queues, and observability are in from day one, not bolted on later.
